📚 Learn

Tragedy of the Commons

Coined by Garrett Hardin (1968): individuals acting in self-interest deplete a shared resource.

🔑 Each person gains full benefit of taking more, but depletion cost is shared by all.

Maximum Sustainable Yield (MSY)

MSY = K × r ÷ 4 — the largest harvest sustainable indefinitely, at population K/2.

📐 K=100, r=0.3 → MSY = 7.5 fish/round for ALL players.

Optimum Sustainable Yield (OSY)

≈ 80% of MSY — conservative buffer for environmental variability.

Nash Equilibrium

Rational strategy: maximize harvest. But collective result = collapse.

AI Strategies

🌿 Sustainable — ≤ fair share of regrowth
💰 Greedy — takes as much as possible
🪞 Copycat — mirrors average human harvest
🧠 Adaptive — scales with population health
🤝 Cooperative — minimum sustainable amount

Real-World Examples

🐟 Overfishing — Grand Banks cod collapsed 1992

🌍 Climate Change — Atmosphere as shared commons

💊 Antibiotic Resistance — Individual benefit, collective harm

Solutions

→ Regulation → Privatization → Community Governance (Ostrom) → Social Norms
